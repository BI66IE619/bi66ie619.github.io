HOW TO PLAY (its not as easy as you think)

Step 1️⃣: So once you click the "Launch Terrarai Web Port" button, 2 tabs will open. There will be a drive link (Content.zip - Google Drive) tab and the main game tab (Terrariun).
Step 2️⃣: Your going to first download the Content.zip folder from the drive link and your gonna extract it.
Step 3️⃣: Next, your gonna go to the Terraria game tab and your gonna click "Copy local assets".
Step 4️⃣: Then your gonna click "Select Terraria Content directory" and choose the "Content" folder you extracted earlier.
Step 5️⃣: Next your gonna wait for the green bar to load and then once its loading, it may or may not show something similar to "game not playing".
Step 6️⃣: So if you do end up seeing this, click the "Play" button on the top right corner.
Step 7️⃣: Then lastly, wait for the game to load and play fullscreen (which is reccommended) and enjoy!
